My Journey at IIT Bombay

Academics Hostel Life Events Friendship

First Experiences

Walking through the gates of IIT Bombay for the first time was surreal. The lush green campus, the towering departments, and the calm of Powai Lake set the tone for what would be an incredible journey.

Academic Achievements

I explored Chemical Engineering deeply, took on challenging projects, and even got involved in consulting and tech initiatives. Each semester taught me not just subjects, but life lessons in discipline and curiosity.

Hostel Life

Hostel life was vibrant — from midnight Maggie runs to group study sessions and hostel fests. It was here I made some of my best memories and closest friends.

Events & Participation

I was a part of NSS, Techfest, and dance nights like Salsa Night. Being a coordinator and aspiring to be a manager taught me leadership, creativity, and collaboration.

Friendships

More than achievements, it was the people that made IITB special — friends who became family, who supported and celebrated with me through highs and lows.
